Everything we‚Äôve looked at so far has had a specific price tag attached to it. Marketing doesn‚Äôt.

It‚Äôs impossible to say how much money you need to spend on marketing. There are, however, some assumptions that we can use to get an estimate.

At the moment, the biggest marketing channel for dropshippers is Facebook, so let‚Äôs look at driving 200 visitors to your store from Facebook. WordStream pegs the average click-through rate (CTR) for Facebook ads at 0.9%. Let‚Äôs assume your ads are good, and you have a 1.0% CTR.

Alright, so to get 200 people to your store, you‚Äôll need your ad to reach 20,000 people. The price for 20,000 impressions will vary depending on who you‚Äôre targeting: Reaching American Facebook users will cost more than reaching Singaporean Facebook users.

(20,000 / 1,000) * 12.50 = $250

Looking at these averages, we land at $250 in Facebook advertising costs to generate five Facebook sales.
